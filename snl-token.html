Your digital assets are safe with us

Our Distributed Ledger (DLT) provides instant and safe money transfers all over the world
DLT creates a decentralized system for trust and transaction validation using consensus, whereby multiple parties agree on a proposed transaction with further approval and automatic update of the ledger
Funds are also protected with a combination of hot and cold storage for further security
Transactions in the SnowLeopard Ecosystem are approved by the proven Stellar Consensus Protocol
