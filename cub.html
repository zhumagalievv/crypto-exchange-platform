Teach them about money early

SnowLeopard Cub account is designed for children to teach them about money from young age. It is fully controlled by parents

Why SnowLeopard Cub is so brilliant

Made for kids, controlled by parents
SnowLeopard Cub features an account, card and app for kids controlled by adults. Get instant notification on spendings, set online and offline limits on payments
Freeze and unfreeze your kid’s card easily via app to control their spending
SnowLeopard Cub cards can’t be used with age-restricted merchants and on age-restricted websites
